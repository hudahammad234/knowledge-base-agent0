# Employee Handbook

## Annual Leave and Vacation Policy

Employees receive 14 annual leave days per calendar year, accrued monthly at a rate of
1.17 days per month. Unused leave may be carried over to the next year up to a maximum
of 5 days. Leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 5 working
days in advance and require manager approval.

## Sick Leave

Employees are entitled to 10 paid sick days per year. A medical certificate is required
for absences longer than 2 consecutive days.

## Maternity and Paternity Leave

Female employees are entitled to 70 days of fully paid maternity leave. Male employees
are entitled to 3 days of paid paternity leave, to be taken within one month of the
child's birth.

## Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week, subject to manager approval and
team coverage requirements. Fully remote arrangements require VP-level sign-off.

## Working Hours

Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Sunday to Thursday, with a one-hour
lunch break. Flexible start times between 8:00 AM and 10:00 AM are permitted.

## Overtime Policy

Overtime is compensated at 1.5x the regular hourly rate for hours worked beyond the
standard 40-hour work week. Overtime must be pre-approved by a direct manager.
